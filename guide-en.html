User Guide — how to connect Zoho, enter your forecast, and read every view of the platform.
AspenView Technology Partners · Sales Forecasting Platform

1What this platform does

A live, browser-based forecasting platform that replaces the manual Excel workbook. It connects to Zoho CRM, lets you layer your forecasting inputs on top of the CRM data, and keeps the whole team looking at the same numbers in real time.

Before — ExcelNow — Platform
Deals copied by hand from ZohoDeals pulled automatically from Zoho
Each person had their own fileEveryone shares one live, auto-synced view
Margin not trackedMargin built in — feeds cash-flow
Static monthly snapshotRolling 3-month forecast that moves with one click
No reliability measureForecast-accuracy bands by horizon

Two kinds of data live together

  • From Zoho (automatic): deal name, account/client, stage, owner (BDE), amount, probability, close date — refreshed every time you Sync.
  • Manual (you enter): monthly headcount per deal, MRR Aureum, win-rate adjustments, margin overrides, the Forecast and Super-Deal flags, and the Super-Deal qualitative assessment.
The key ideaThe manual inputs are what turn raw CRM data into a real, decision-ready forecast.

2Getting in

  1. Open the platform URL in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Click Sign in with Google.
  3. Choose your @aspenview.com account. Only @aspenview.com accounts are allowed.
  4. You land on the Overview tab.

The header (top-right)

IndicatorMeaning
Sync · LiveYour data is connected and shared. Saving… = pushing a change; Live (updated by …) = a teammate's change just arrived.
ZohoConnected / Disconnected — click to connect or disconnect Zoho.
Name · Log OutYour identity and sign-out.
Shared in real timeEverything you edit is shared. If the manager changes a number, you see it within seconds — and vice-versa.

3Connecting Zoho

The Zoho token lasts about one hour, so you'll reconnect occasionally.

  1. In the header, click the Zoho · Disconnected pill.
  2. On Zoho, click Accept to authorize read access to Deals and Accounts.
  3. You return; the pill turns green: Zoho · Connected.

Pulling the deals

  1. Go to Aureum Demand Forecast.
  2. Click ↻ Sync from Zoho (top-right).
  3. A summary shows how many deals were pulled, matched, skipped, and how many manual deals are preserved.
  4. Click Accept. The list refreshes with the latest from Zoho.

Which deals come in — only these stages (everything except Closed Lost):

SQL - Solution Discovery · Stage 2 (WTW) · Commercial Design · Recruitment (Path 2.1) · P.S (Path 2.2) · Pricing · Paper Process / Contracting · Closed Won

Your work is safe on re-syncMonthly headcount, MRR Aureum, win-rate edits, and the Forecast/Super flags stay attached to each deal. Only the Zoho fields (stage, amount, owner) get refreshed.

4The tabs, one by one

4.1 — Overview

Your executive snapshot: KPI cards (deals, 3-month headcount, adjusted revenue, margin $), the Anchor month picker, the rolling 3-month window table, and the Forecast Reliability bands (M0 / M+1 / M+2).

4.2 — Aureum Demand Forecast (the master list)

Where everything starts. Every Zoho deal in the allowed stages is one row, grouped by account so all deals of a client sit together.

Search & filters: the search box filters by deal, client, or BDE. The BDE / Stage / Status dropdowns are multi-select — pick two BDEs at once, etc. A badge shows the count; "✕ Clear" resets a filter.

4.3 — 3 mo. revenue (the rolling forecast)

Shows only the deals you checked "Fcst". For each, across the 3-month window: HC, Proj. Rev (HC × MRR/head), Adj. Rev (× Win %), Margin $ (× Margin %). Totals roll up at the bottom and in three summary cards.

This is the number the company seesIt only contains deals you deliberately flagged — nothing leaks in by accident.

4.4 — Forecast Accuracy

Measures how reliable the forecast has been — not a way to grade anyone. The platform snapshots the forecast monthly; when a month closes you Enter Actuals, and the bands recompute as mean ± standard deviation per horizon. Defaults until history builds: M0 = 80% ±5, M+1 = 75% ±10, M+2 = 30% ±20.

4.5 — Super-Deals

The Aureum-tier program assessment. Each program is a row with editable, color-coded dropdowns: Deal Confidence, Start Readiness, Readiness Gap, Speed Sensitivity, Ramp Duration, First Phase Timing, Phase Risk Concentration, Program Horizon, Deployment Map. Edit Peak HC / Phase 1 / Activation / Notes with ✎. A deal-level rollup below sums every deal flagged "Super".

4.6 — Fulfillment View

Headcount by deal-type (Active Roles + Super-Deal Roles) for delivery/recruiting prioritization. Filter by BDE and search.

4.7 — MRR Forecast 2026

Adjusted revenue by client across all 12 months. Read it by quarter by grouping the relevant month columns (Jan–Mar = Q1, etc.). Search by client.

4.8 — Active Customers — Actuals

Only customers currently producing revenue (Active / Expanding / Aureum): current headcount, MRR per head, monthly $. Filter by BDE and status.

4.9 — Margin Rules

The margin assumptions that feed cash-flow. Margin resolves in this order:

  1. Per-deal override (the Margin % typed on the deal) →
  2. Super-Deal rule (if flagged Super) →
  3. Stage rule (matches the deal's stage) →
  4. Default fallback.

4.10 — Settings

Zoho integration & field mapping, data Export / Import (JSON) for backup, reset, and the daily-workflow reminder.

5How the forecast actually moves

ZOHO ──(Sync)──► Aureum Demand Forecast │ you enter: Monthly HC + MRR Aureum (if Zoho blank) + Win % + Margin % you check: Forecast ☑ Super ☑ │ ▼ Forecast ☑ deals ──► 3 mo. revenue (HC × MRR/head × Win% × Margin%) Super ☑ deals ──► Super-Deals rollup │ ▼ Overview KPIs + Forecast Accuracy bands + MRR Forecast

The levers you pull: Monthly HC drives projected revenue · MRR (Zoho wins if set, else Aureum) is the price per deal · Win % discounts by probability · Margin % produces the cash-flow number · Anchor month slides the window · the Forecast/Super checkboxes decide what shows up where.

7Tips & troubleshooting

SymptomFix
Revenue shows $0No MRR Zoho and no MRR Aureum — enter an MRR Aureum value or set the Amount in Zoho.
A deal isn't in 3 mo. revenueCheck its Fcst box and confirm it has headcount in the anchor window.
Sign-in "not authorized"You used a non-@aspenview.com account. Switch accounts.
Zoho sync fails (401)The 1-hour token expired. Click the Zoho pill → reconnect.
A deal shows ⚠ none for BDENo deal in that account has an owner in Zoho. Assign one in Zoho, then re-sync.
Changes not showing elsewhereConfirm both screens show Sync · Live; hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R).

8Backup

Firestore keeps the shared copy, but export a backup periodically: Settings → ⬇ Export all data (JSON). To restore: Settings → ⬆ Import data (JSON).
